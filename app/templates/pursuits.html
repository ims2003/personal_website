I've devoted much of my own time to my hobbies and pursuing excellence, community, and nature through them. If I could be anywhere in the world it would be outside, doing one of these activities, with some of the people I love.

Running

I started running in High School, I did so after being forced by my parents join an afterschool sport to keep fit. At some point along the way I fell in love with it. I continued running into university with the Vancouver Thunderbirds for 3 years. After a series of injuries and inconsistency I took a step away from competitive racing to fall in love with the sport again. Some of my proudest achievements and closest friends come from the grass and track.

Vancouver Thunderbirds Post Secondary, 2025

Canadian U20 Cross Country Nationals for Team British Columba, 2022

Triathlon

I began triathlon after a close friend of mine found success in it after leaving running behind. To me, it allowed me to be part of a collegiate team and fall back in love with competing. Throughout my short time in the sport I've grown fond of both swiming and cycling. There is something special about the focus and silence required to improve in swimming. Cycling has become my favorite however, I deeply enjoy efforts outdoors exploring the natural beauty of Vancouver. I've grown particularly fond of climbing and time trialing, they take much of the 'game' away from cycling and challenge your fitness, consistency, and heart instead.
